RESEARCH HIGHLIGHTS

Cutting-edge research projects with real-world applications

AI for Sustainable Agriculture

Machine learning models to optimize crop yields while reducing water consumption and pesticide use in partnership with local farmers.

Status: Phase 2 - Field Testing
Partners: Agricultural Department, Local Farmers Co-op
Impact: 30% reduction in water use, 25% increase in yields

Microplastic Detection System

Affordable sensor technology for detecting microplastics in water sources, developed for deployment in rural communities.

Status: Prototype Development
Partners: Environmental Science Dept, Water Authority
Impact: Real-time water quality monitoring

Social Enterprise Mapping

GIS-based platform to identify and connect social enterprises working on poverty alleviation and economic development.

Status: Data Collection Phase
Partners: NGO Network, Municipal Government
Impact: 100+ organizations mapped and connected
